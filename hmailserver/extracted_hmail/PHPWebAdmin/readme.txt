CONFIGURING PHPWEBADMIN
-----------------------

1) Copy config-dist.php to config.php.
2) Open config.php.
3) Find "CHANGEME" and change it to reflect your system.
   There are examples in the bottom of config.php if you aren't 
   sure what to write.
   When you enter directory names, use / as folder seperator instead of \.
5) Point your browser to the root-url location in the config.php
